People, Entity, Organization, Government etc. who are directly related to the impact of the security of your organization. Commonly referred to as malicious actors

Attributes

  • They could be internal (hired by your company or your company may not even know) or external (working from outsite to damage your system).

  • They might have low budget or back with high funding.

  • They might be script kiddie, expert or somewhere between them.

Motivation

  • Data Exfiltration
  • Service Disruption
  • Expose
  • Money
  • Revenge
  • Ethical
  • Blackmail
  • Fun
  • Philosophical/Political/War

Advanced Persistent Threat (APT)

  • Government with full resources comming after you.

  • Hactivists comming after you.

  • Group of malicious actors (all specialized in certain field like: script writing, hacking, manipulating, etc) comming after you.

  • Expert backed by huge organization and money or government agency.

Insider Threat

  • Malicious Actor inside org.

  • Knows where and how to hit.

  • Having Insider institutional knowledge.

  • Using org resources against themselves.

  • (Recruited by yourself, but you don't know).

Organized Crime

  • Professional Criminals mostly motivated by money.

  • Very Sophisticated in a sense best hack money can buy.

  • Group of malicious actors (all specialized in certain field like: script writing, hacking, manipulating, selling, handling customers, etc).

Shadow IT

  • Working around the internal IT organization.

  • Build own infrastructure.

  • May be limited to how much resources organization provide.

  • Attacking own organization, without proper backout plan (they have been instructed to go full and final).
