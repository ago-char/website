Segmenting the Network

You may want to divide your network into different pieces for many reasons. This may be Physically Seperating with different switches, Logical seperation with VLANs or Virtual Seperation with VMs.

Reasons

Performance.

  • You may wanna seperate your high band apps in seperate network.

  • Provide high bandwidth to the network.

  • This won't be effected by slow throughput, or you can't compromise speed here.

Security

  • You may want to restrict certain netwrok being accessed directly by end users.

  • Such as : Databases may not be directly available to users but only app level network and users will ask app level network to access database on their behalf.

Compliance

  • You can refer this to as high level security.

  • includes implementing adequate security controls and measures to safeguard data against unauthorized access, cyber-attacks, and breaches.

  • broad framework for managing and implementing risk management governance for government agencies and business stakeholders.

ACLs (Access Control Lists)

  • Creating Allow/Deny List based on :

    • IP (src or dest)
    • MAC (src or dest)
    • DNS (src or dest)
    • Port
    • admin or normal user
    • Application
    • Service
    • Time
    • Certificate

  • Be careful ! Don't lock yourself out while creating these ACL, which may prevent you from editing these lists in future

ACLs in Windows

  • Apply in combo with hash

  • Apply with certificate

  • Apply on the basis of path i.e. only apps from path : C:/myapp will run or only app from path D:/maliciousapp won't run

  • Apply on the basis of private or public network zones
