Hardware Vulnerabilities

  • IOT devices, with their ease of connect to the network has grown new scope of security.

  • Lot of these devices don't give us way to get inside their firmware or OS.

  • An unupdated/outdated device could be entry point for an attacker.

End of life

  • but sill may continue supporting the product.

End of Service Life (EOSL)

  • stop selling product.

  • no support is available.

  • no patches will be released.

  • may have premium support function or premium patch function that will be specific to you.

What after EOSL

  • You may consider replacing the device.

  • Sometimes, it is hard as it could be key part of your production.

  • So, you should really consider the risk balancing on if you contine or to throw it away.

  • Make it more sucure by adding extra layer of security, making as isolate as possible, keep under the eys of security personnel and ultimately replace.
