Supply Chain Vulnerabilities

  • Chain of **Raw Materials -> Supplier -> Manufacturor -> Distributor -> Customer (we)

  • One exploit in this chain can fully damage.

  • Generally we trust supplier so we don't take this in central issue, when new system or device is being delivered.

Service Providers

When you are managing your systems on you own, you know exact security posture but what if your Network, Devices, Administration etc. is managed by the 3rd party Service Provider ? Obviously you trust them in first place to give them access, but this must be restrictive.

  • Service Provider should be responsible for all security concerns in the system.

  • If Attacker could gain access to the service provider, they will now have all your data.

  • So, it is must to perform security audit with these 3rd party provides.

  • The Aduit is related to Hey what are the procedures you are applying to manage and secure our data/system ?

  • You must be familier with this chain in the 3rd party provider.

Hardware Providers

  • Shoud you trust your hardware providers ?

  • Limit your trust to some vendors in the market.

  • Policies and Procedures for aquisition and implementation of these hardware .

  • Test before deploying even if you are using vendor specifc hardware.

  • This is important because, you have have been provided Counterfited Hardware i.e just looking similar but different configuration or OS running inside.
