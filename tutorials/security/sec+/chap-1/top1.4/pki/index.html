Public Key Infrastructure

  • It (PKI) is very board term in cryptography.

  • Commony refers to as policies and procedures, hardware and softwares, peope that is responsible for creating, distributing, managing, storing, revoking, destroying and performing other processess associated with digital cerificates for digital security.

  • This seems easy but this is a big task that requrire a lot of planning

    • What type of encryption be used ?
    • Who are our partners, what they are using ?
    • How to manage these amount of keys ?
    • How to keep them safe and secure ?

  • Also refers to the binding of public keys to people or device

    • Generally done by CA (Certificate Authority)
    • Way to trust people or device on the network
