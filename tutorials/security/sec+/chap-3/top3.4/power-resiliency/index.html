Power Resiliency

  • We spend significant amount of time in designing our tech environment, but we also need to take care of power supply to run those equipments.
  • Power Resiliency is the backup plain if main electricity or power outage or cut happens.

UPS (Uniniterruptible Power Supply)

  • Works perfectly for short term power outage or in situation of blackouts (no power), brownouts (low voltage) and surges (high voltages).

  • There are different type of UPS :

    • Offline/Standby UPS will auto on whenever main power is off.
    • Line-interactive UPS will constantly monitor and increase voltage in case of brownouts.
    • On-line/Double Conversion UPS will only work on battery power of your UPS i.e. no main line will be use, this eradicate the situation of being internal switch turn on and off although it happens automatically.

Generator

  • Works for large outage.

  • You may wanna use UPS although you have generator, because it takes a while to shift from main line to generator line.
