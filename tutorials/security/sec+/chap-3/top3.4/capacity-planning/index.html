Capacity Planning

  • It is always a difficult task to predict the Demand-Supply.

  • If demand is high and you are not able to supply on time, this will affect your revenue and potentially cause outage.

  • And if the supply is high, you are paying more than you need to, so will directly affect your future capacity and profit also.

So, while designing your platform or company, you will need to design in flexible way.

Technology Engineering

  • Before designing, you will need such a plan in technology building which must be scalable. Not all type of technology can scale, so be careful while designing.

  • You may initially wanna use load balancers for your servers, culster your servers or array your storage. So that you can easily add or remove any computers behind the scene without end user noticing.

  • If you are using cloud, you may wanna use load balancers there too or use multiple on demand cloud providers' services, instead of using static.

Underlying Infrastructure

  • You should be aware of what online or offline platform to choose, this means if to use physical site or to build on cloud.

  • As, building site physical required people, equipments, location, building, etc. this may not be scalable now.

  • But also, you may not want to put everything online specifically those which are secret or private for your own company.

  • So, you design for internal use be at physical near your office, but for your clients or end user, things and app may be deployed online on cloud.

Employees or People

  • They are tough to handle with flexibility and scability.

  • You may need to hire or fire.

  • For hiring, you require additional process of recruiment, tranning, etc.

  • If you fire, you may gain "revenue of paying them", but may lose "their knowledge that your training had provided". So, it always challenging.
